Spotify

Spotify is a digital music provider which has many available platforms to use, including desktop software and mobile application. The application has a simple login form with outstanding error alert, which is easy to understand during the first time of using the app. It also provides alternative signup and login with the Facebook account, that users do not need to fill their information when signup or login into the application.

Nonetheless, the size of fonts is small and low colour contrast with background on some part of the application. This would be a problem for the user with vision deficiency and elder people, these may not readable for them. And the app does not provide the increased text size, but users can use zoom in accessibility feature to increase their vision, both zooming and the colour filter.

In addition, Spotify has perfectly supported VoiceOver in both desktop application and mobile application. It read aloud every contents and button understandable with correctly ordering structure.

The evaluation and test were take place on iOS version 10.3.3 (14G60) in iPhone 6s model MKQN2TH/A and MacBook Air (Early 2014) with macOS High Sierra (10.13.2)
